01 / About

Bridging the gap between technology & business

I'm a Technology Program Manager with a background in multi-asset trading, fintech integrations, and authentication workflows. I thrive in complex environments where technology meets finance — translating ambiguous business goals into structured technical programs that actually ship.

I've spent the last nine years at FXCM and Tradu, growing from a client-facing financial services representative to SVP of Technology Program Management. Along the way I've led support teams across three continents, spearheaded the Bulgarian hub from scratch, and eventually crossed into the development organisation to oversee the trading platforms used by hundreds of thousands of traders worldwide.

I maintain a genuine interest in the technical side of what I manage. Outside of work I build side projects and experiments — you'll find them below. Staying close to the code makes me a better PM.
